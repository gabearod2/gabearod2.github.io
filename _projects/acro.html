AcroRL

This project aimed to leverage bidirectional thrust to complete compact inversion maneuvers. Due to difficult actuator dynamics, it is difficult to maintain altitude while you wait for the rotors to generate thrust in the opposite direction. We explored a variety of approaches to complete the desired task. There were three main ones:

- First-order model-based reinforcement learning
- Model-free reinforcement learning
- Trajectory optimization with geometric control
